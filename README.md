ColdVault is a simple ultra lightweight disk cleaner written purely in Go. 
<img width="631" height="583" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/372fffa3-84ef-4da1-b25b-f21c162c672c" />
After build, you can add the files you want the to be monitored and it's done. It does not erase your files, it simply zips them and moves to the mounted cloud storage. (Onedrive, Yandex Disk etc) 
And you won't even notice your files are gone since it automatically creates shortcuts to your files. If you need them again, you can find a bat file with the same name as your original file. 
When you click, it brings back your original file from the cloud. 

I'm a historian and I download many books, just check what I need, close them and never open it for ages. But I also hesitate erasing them, since you never know when you need it. 

Thus, ColdVault simply helps you clean your disk without any hesitation. It works automatically everyday at 11am, cleans your files in the background without you even noticing and closes in a second.
